Project Overview

I love sharing my passion for STEM with others! On my YouTube channel "SciPK", I create engaging and informative videos that explore various STEM projects, concepts, history, and people. My goal is to make STEM loved by the masses through enjoyable, yet educational content. The types of content I share are:

  • Projects: Where I make something cool and discuss it.
  • Video Essays: Where I explain a STEM subject, such as history, a person, an object, or a phenomena.

Backstory

My cousin (AbrahamThePharmacist on YouTube) used to make Persian comedy skits when I was a kid. I lived in England briefly, and he'd put me in his videos. After he graduated medical school, he shifted his channel to be medical focused. He inspired me to start my own channel around STEM topics. When he would include me in his comedy skits, I learned that I enjoy videography. My love for public speaking comes from my time doing theater in high school. Granted, it was for the easy A, but I found I enjoyed being on stage. Combining these two skills with my passion for Engineering, I started SciPK to share my love for STEM with others.
