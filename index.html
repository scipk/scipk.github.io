AIAA SDSU Student Branch History

üë• Parham Khodadi, Ava Cook, Xiaofeng Liu

üìÖ Presenting at AIAA SciTech 2026

This paper conveys the history of the AIAA student branch at San Diego State University, founded in Spring 1963, and pays tribute to all those who made an impact in and on the club. The SDSU AIAA chapter serves as a hub for students, engineers, researchers, professors, and industry professionals, supporting collaboration with major aerospace companies. Through organizing technical symposia, projects, student competitions, networking events, and educational outreach, AIAA at SDSU strengthens the region's leadership in aerospace technology. This paper highlights the people, events, and projects that have sustained the student branch through its many challenges over six decades.
