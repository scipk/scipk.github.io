AIAA SDSU Student Branch History

Parham Khodadi, Ava Cook, Xiaofeng Liu

Presenting at AIAA SciTech 2026

This paper conveys the history of the AIAA student branch at San Diego State University, founded in Spring 1963, and pays tribute to all those who made an impact in and on the club. The SDSU AIAA chapter serves as a hub for students, engineers, researchers, professors, and industry professionals, supporting collaboration with major aerospace companies.
